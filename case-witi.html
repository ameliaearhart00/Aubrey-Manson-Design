Women in Technology International

Responsive Website Redesign Solution

OVERVIEW

About the Project

The purpose of Women In Technology International’s redesign and rebranding was to demonstrate the organization’s mission and increase membership with prominent CTA buttons and highlights of member benefits. Responsive designs were built for its homepage, membership page, and sign-up module.

PROJECT BRIEF

Identifying The Challenge


Question: If there was a central place to access resources, career help and mentorships for Women in Tech, would this lessen the inequality gap?

BRIEF

As we are all aware, the disparity between genders in tech exists and statistics are showing these disparities are actually growing. Women in Technology International is already on the forefront of helping women in tech advance in their fields, so having these resources made more prominent on their website would:

  1. Help the organization's mission
  2. Increase awareness to the lack of equality
  3. Close the gender gap by easily providing women with the career resources they need.
